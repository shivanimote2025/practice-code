1. Masala Bread Toast (5 mins)

Ingredients:
  • 2–3 bread slices
  • small onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 tbsp curd
  • Salt, chilli powder
  • Butter/oil
Recipe:
  1. Mix onion, chilli, curd, salt, chilli powder.
  2. Spread on bread.
  3. Toast on tawa with little butter.
  4. Cook till golden.

2. Instant Veg Sandwich (5 mins)

Ingredients:
  • Bread slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Cucumber slices
  • Onion slices
  • Green chutney / ketchup
  • Salt & pepper
Recipe:
  1. Spread chutney.
  2. Layer veggies.
  3. Add salt & pepper.
  4. Toast or eat raw.

3. Bread Pizza (5 mins)

Ingredients:
  • Bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Chopped veggies
  • Grated cheese
  • Oregano
Recipe:
  1. Spread sauce on bread.
  2. Add veggies & cheese.
  3. Microwave 1 min or tawa cook 2–3 mins.
